Our History

Get to Know Us

In operation for over 50 years, Empire State Health was designed with the idea of patient health and safety first. Our practice started with a small group of young practitioners devoted to providing the highest quality care, safety, and hospitality to patients. Our practice is built on diversity and accurate health treament.

Over the decades, we've expanded our specialties to meet the needs of a wider variety of patients, refining our medical knowledge and understanding of procedures, and embracing new technology in the hospital. Our commitment to patient health is unrivaled, with an extraordinary track record of patient confidence and recovery time.



As we look towards the future, we predict massive growth in the range of medical specialties our practice offers. With the continued hard-work of our dedicated staff, supportive stakeholders, and wonderful community we are confident that Empire State Health will continue to thrive and make a lasting impact on the quality of health of our clientele for years to come.

Our Mission

Committed to Health

At Empire State Health, our mission is to ensure the health in our community by providing quality examinations, promoting physical fitness, and performing precise surgicial procedures. Boasting a wide variety of medical departments, we aim to provide the utmost quality in consultations, hospitality, and health services to our clientele.

Our Values

Important to Us

To best serve the dynamic needs of our communities, we strive to constantly improve our staff's variety of skills and seek out collaboration with professionals from all types of medical backgrounds. We always prioritize delivering the highest level of care, contstant innovation, and responsiveness. By fostering this culture of diverse medical knowledge, we are committed to adapting to the unique needs of those we serve, now and in the future.
